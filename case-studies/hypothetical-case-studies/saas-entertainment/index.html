Client Overview

Company Name: StreamWise Inc. (Hypothetical Name)

Industry: SaaS Entertainment Platform (Streaming Service)

Location: United States

Annual Revenue: $1 Billion+

Objective: Drive subscriber growth, optimize customer retention, and expand global market reach.

Challenges

  • High subscriber churn due to inconsistent engagement and insufficient personalization.
  • Rising acquisition costs with diminishing returns on ad campaigns.
  • Difficulty reaching international audiences with tailored messaging.
  • Disconnected data sources hampering actionable insights.

Strategic Interventions

  • Customer journey mapping and personalized onboarding campaigns.
  • Geo-targeted PPC campaigns and AI-driven bidding strategies.
  • Retention marketing with loyalty programs and behavioral targeting.
  • Centralized data integration for real-time analytics.
  • Localized content strategies with regional influencer partnerships.

Results Achieved

  • 50% global subscriber growth within 12 months.
  • 25% churn rate reduction through personalized retention campaigns.
  • 35% revenue increase from 10 new international markets.
  • 60% engagement boost with improved onboarding and loyalty programs.
